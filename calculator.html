Why Use a Calculator?

Understanding the numbers on paper is one thing. Seeing them visualized over time helps you:

  • Plan your budget – Know exactly when money will arrive

  • Make informed decisions – Understand the true cost of borrowing
  • Track your progress – Compare actual disbursements to your plan

What If My Situation Is Different?

Transfer Students

The calculator can account for mid-year transfers and adjusted disbursement schedules.

Part-Time Students

If you're enrolled less than full-time, your loan amounts and disbursement timing may differ. The calculator can help you model this.

Graduate Students

Graduate student loans follow the same disbursement rules, but with different loan limits and interest rates. The calculator handles both undergraduate and graduate scenarios.
